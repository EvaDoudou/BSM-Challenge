What is a Growth Mindset?

There are many aspects to having a Growth Mindset, check below for more information.

Extra Info

Grow Through Repetition

With a growth mindset, an individual believes that he can develop
a skill through practice, as long as he doesn't give up, he would be successful.

Failure Isn't The End

Those with a growth mindset does not view failure as the end. They view it as a learning experience, to not make the same mistake twice.
